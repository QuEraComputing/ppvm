Expand description

Correlated two-qubit loss channel.

Required Methods§

fn correlated_loss_channel( &mut self, addr0: usize, addr1: usize, p: [<T as Config>::Coeff; 3], )

Apply a correlated loss channel to qubits at addr0 and addr1.

The three probabilities are:

  • p[0]: The probability of losing both qubits simultaneously when both of them are in the qubit subspace.
  • p[1]: The probability of losing either one qubit when both of them are in the qubit subspace.
  • p[2]: The probability of losing one qubit when the other one has already been lost prior to the channel.
